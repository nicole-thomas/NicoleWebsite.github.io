Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 slice cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Directions

  1. Preheat griddle to medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place buttered side of bread on griddle. Top bread with cheese slice.
  4. Top cheese with remaining bread slice with buttered side on top.
  5. Cook for a few minutes. Flip over and cook until golden brown on other side.
  6. Serve with a smile!
